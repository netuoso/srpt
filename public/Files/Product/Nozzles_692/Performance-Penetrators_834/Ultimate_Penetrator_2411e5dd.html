Ultimate Panetrator
Heavy duty nozzle chosen by contractors to clear
sludge, mud, and debris from pipe bottom.  Can be used to
create a path to center a flushing nozzle in severe buildup, minimizing
water consumption and cleaning time.  Replaceable carbide inserts for
long service life.
 
 
 
12º rear jets on most sizes.
Rear jets are 15º on nozzle for hose H.
Forward jet standard.
Four forward jet (one at 0º, three at 7º) available
on request for hose size H.
Color is safety orange.
