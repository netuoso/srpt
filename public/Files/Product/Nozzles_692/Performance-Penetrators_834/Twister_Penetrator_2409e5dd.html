Twister Penetrator
A front rotating (twisting) water jet impacts and removes grease, soap,
ice and hair roots on contact.  At 65 gpm - 2000 psi the forward penetrator flows
10 gpm.  Six carbide thrust jets sweep the debris or sludge.  Manufactured of
heat treated alloy steel, surface coated for corrosion resistance with a polished
carbide water jet and stainless steel internals.
